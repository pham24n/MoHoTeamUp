Connect, collaborate, create

Moho TeamUp is an initiative that aims to help Mount Holyoke College students create meaningful technical projects.
On Moho TeamUp, you can:

  • Propose your project to the community and attract like-minded peers
  • Connect and join with others on their technical projects

Why should you create a project?

  • It's a great learning opportunity to improve your skills and explore new technologies
  • It's also a chance to unleash your creativity and realize the awesome ideas that you've been dreaming about
  • Moreover, you get to connect and work with other passionate and talented Computer Science students
  • A side project will also be an interesting point on your resume where you can showcase your skills and interests to the recruiters
  • Above all, it will be an enjoyable journey where you will be working on what you are passtionate about!

Let's get started

What are you waiting for? Let's make the most out of your summer by working on a meaningful technical project with your peers!

  • If you already have an idea, propose your project here and we'll help you find your teammates.
  • Don't have an idea yet? No worries! Check out our Resources page to find out more about how to find inspiration for your project idea.
  • If you're looking for a project to join, view our project list here.
